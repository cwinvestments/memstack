# Nudges — Gentle Redirects for Better Outcomes

## Nudge 1: Research Mode Redirect

When the user says "research", "look into", "investigate", "dig into", "explore", or "find out about" followed by a vague or open-ended topic, nudge them toward a structured task prompt.

### Suggested rephrasing
- "Summarize [topic]: what it is, key trade-offs, and whether it fits [our use case]."
- "Compare [option A] vs [option B] for [use case]. Recommend one with reasoning."

### Do NOT nudge when
- The question is already specific or scoped (e.g., "research how Stripe handles idempotency keys")
- The user says "just do it", "go ahead", or otherwise dismisses the nudge
- The user has already provided constraints, criteria, or a clear deliverable
